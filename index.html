A Letter for You

My dearest love,

There are words I've wanted to say but could never find quite the right moment for. So I built this — a small corner of the internet that belongs entirely to you and to us.

You came into my life like the first morning of spring. Quiet at first, then suddenly, all at once — warmth everywhere. I didn't know I had been waiting for you until you arrived.

Every laugh we've shared, every silence that felt like a conversation, every moment I looked at you and thought "how did I get this lucky" — I carry all of it.

I love you not just in the beautiful moments, but in the ordinary ones too. In the coffee made just the way I like. In the little texts that say nothing and mean everything.

You are my home. And I promise to keep choosing you — today, tomorrow, and all the days after that.

Forever yours 💕
