A Letter for You

My dearest love,

There are things I've felt so deeply that words have always seemed too small for them. But I wanted to try — because you deserve to know, in the most deliberate and thoughtful way I can manage, what you truly mean to me.

You came into my life like a song I didn't know I'd been waiting to hear. Softly at first, then all at once — filling every corner, every quiet room, every early morning. Before you, I didn't know what it felt like to be truly seen.

I love you in the grand gestures and in the small ones. In the way you laugh without thinking. In the way your eyes go soft when you're lost in thought. In every ordinary Tuesday that becomes extraordinary because you're in it.

You are my safe place and my greatest adventure, all at once. And I promise you this — I will keep choosing you, not just today but in every version of tomorrow that comes.

"I will love you till the end of time…"

Forever & always yours 💕
